What documents do I need?

Federal Documents

A copy of a Federal IRS document that has the congregation’s name and EIN assigned to the organization (NOT your parent organization, if applicable).

For example: Acceptable documents include, but are not limited to:

  1. Form 941 OR
  2. Payroll tax receipts OR
  3. EIN issuance letter OR
  4. Another document that has the congregation’s name and Federal EIN
State / Religious Documents
  1. The Church listing in the parent Church National Directory OR
  2. A copy of the State Registration OR
  3. A copy of the Sales & Use Tax Exemption issued by the State
