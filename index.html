How to get listed on GuideStar

Get started

Make it easy for donors to find you!

First, we'll help you get listed on guidestar.org. Don't worry, it's easy and will let you connect with donors and our partners — major giving platforms, employee giving plans, and online platforms — to share your up-to-date information.

Then, you need to earn a Bronze seal to stay listed on GuideStar and share the best information with our partners. We make it easy to come back annually to renew your listing.

Get listed and approved to update

Step 1 is get started with getting your organization listed on guidestar.org, and getting approved to update the organization's information.

Earn a Bronze seal

Step 2 is updating your information on your GuideStar Nonprofit Profile, and earning what we call the "Bronze" seal.

Listed and shared!

Step 3 is seeing your information go out to all of our partner networks. Remember to come back annually to update your information and keep your listing current (it will expire after 12 months).

How long is my listing good for?

We require that you update your profile at least once per calendar year in order to keep the information current. GuideStar.org is your one-stop-shop for keeping your details current -- we'll do all the work to then keep our partner networks up to date so you can receive donations.

What information do I need?

Federal Documents

A copy of a Federal IRS document that has the congregation’s name and EIN assigned to the organization (NOT your parent organization, if applicable).

For example: Acceptable documents include, but are not limited to:

  1. Form 941 OR
  2. Payroll tax receipts OR
  3. EIN issuance letter OR
  4. Another document that has the congregation’s name and Federal EIN
State / Religious Documents
  1. The Church listing in the parent Church National Directory OR
  2. A copy of the State Registration OR
  3. A copy of the Sales & Use Tax Exemption issued by the State
